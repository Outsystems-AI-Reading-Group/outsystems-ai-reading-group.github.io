AI Reading Group

Every Friday 14:00-15:00 GMT+0

We are a research group that gathers every friday to discuss one research paper in the area of AI.

We are interested in any kind of AI applied to any kind of data (text, images, graphs, video, tabular, you name it!).

Join the AI Reading Group Army and/or Contact Us to present something!
